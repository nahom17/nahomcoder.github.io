About Me

Nahom Tesfamichael

Hello! My name is Nahom Tesfamichael, a dedicated and passionate software developer based in the Netherlands. Originally from Eritrea, I have been living in this beautiful country for nearly 8 years, pursuing my dreams and expanding my knowledge in the field of software development.

I am currently studying software development at Alfa-College Groningen, where I am honing my skills and gaining valuable experience. My journey has been both challenging and rewarding, shaping me into a resilient and determined individual.

As a developer, I specialize in PHP and the Laravel framework, and I am proficient in various other technologies including Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, and Livewire. I am always eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills.

In addition to coding, I have a passion for photography and content creation. I enjoy capturing moments of beauty and inspiration through my lens and sharing my experiences and tutorials on YouTube.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and get in touch if you would like to collaborate or learn more about my work.
